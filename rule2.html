RULE 1: Use the arrow keys to move your character and to enter into battle, all you need to have to do is run into any tree, and the battle will begin.

RULE 2: Click on attack if you want to attack the enemy, or heal to heal your health.

RULE 3: Click anywhere after battle ends to go back to the forest area.

RULE 4: There are 6 different enemy types, try to win against them all.

RULE 5: You can only quit the game from the forest area, and the quit button will be pretty obvious
